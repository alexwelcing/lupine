## Quality Assessment

### Strengths
- **Comprehensive method coverage:** Classical potentials (EAM, MEAM) and ML approaches both represented
- **Validated operators:** All operators include Python implementations with error bounds
- **Physical grounding:** Operators based on Born stability criteria, volume-error scaling, and bonding character metrics
- **Risk mitigation:** High-risk scenarios (stability-margin) have explicit constraints

### Limitations
- **BCC gap:** BCC metals (Fe, Mo, W) underrepresented in corpus; only Cauchy-pressure operator supports these
- **Temporal bias:** Literature skewed toward 2015-2023 ML advances; classical EAM coverage from earlier periods
- **Validation pending:** Operators have theoretical error bounds; empirical validation against test sets needed
- **Scope restricted:** FCC-centric; HCP and other structures not addressed

---

## Known Limitations

1. **ML potential integration:** Pipeline designed for classical potentials; ML potentials may not benefit from corrections (already <1% error)

2. **Volume error threshold:** Volume-scaling operator fails if classical volume error exceeds 5%

3. **Stability-margin sensitivity:** May produce unstable corrections for potentials near mechanical instability

4. **Cauchy-pressure C44 dependency:** Some EAM potentials have systematic C44 errors affecting Cauchy pressure calculations

5. **Transferability unverified:** Operator accuracy predictions based on literature; empirical validation with held-out potentials required
