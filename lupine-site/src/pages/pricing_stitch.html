Frequently Asked Inquiries

Do you offer academic or institutional discounts?

Yes. We believe in supporting fundamental research. Verified academic institutions are eligible for a 40% reduction on the Research tier. Please contact our liaison team with your institutional email.

How is data sovereignty handled on the Research tier?

All simulation data on the Research tier is siloed within a private cloud tenant (AWS or GCP, based on your preference). Data is encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256 standards.

Can we integrate existing LAMMPS scripts?

The Research and Enterprise tiers fully support direct import of LAMMPS input scripts. Our parser translates standard potentials and boundary conditions into our proprietary framework automatically.
