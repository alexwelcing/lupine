Do you offer academic or non-profit discounts? add

Yes. Verified academic institutions and 501(c)(3) organizations are eligible for a 60% discount on the Research tier. Contact our academic relations team with your .edu credentials.

How is data sovereignty handled for international labs? add

Enterprise clients can specify data residency in 14 global regions. For ITAR-compliant projects, we provide physically isolated US-East sovereign regions.

Can we integrate our proprietary force fields? add

The Research and Enterprise tiers support custom Python hooks for proprietary potential functions, allowing you to run Lupine simulations on your private IP.

Is Lupine SOC2 Type II compliant? add

Our Enterprise infrastructure is audited annually. SOC2 reports and penetration test results are available to Enterprise customers under NDA.

What happens to my data if I cancel? add

You maintain 100% ownership. Upon cancellation, you have a 60-day window to export all simulation ledgers and atomic trajectories in standard .xyz or .json formats.

Does the Research tier include compute credits? add

The Research annual fee includes 5,000 GPU-hours on our H100 clusters. Additional hours are billed at a flat rate of $0.80/hour with no ingress/egress fees.
